## Athmar Park Newsletter
### Mission
To inform, organize, and entertain the residents of Athmar Park, and to contribute to a culture of community in the neighborhood.
### Quarterly Newsletter Production Process
1. Copy the template(https://docs.google.com/document/d/1IZU1C5VykXYuIgQ9lAitPeJBZlljFtzpy1zGdgMyOHg/edit#)

### Regular Pieces
- Word from the President
- Association updates
- Updates from comittee updates
- Updates on current issues
- Events
## Process
- Set up automated reminder asking contributors for contributions
  - APNA President
  - Contributors
  - political parties
- Notify everyone of due dates, meetings, etc.
- Schedule one or more writing sessions

### Notes
Approximate word count: 500/pg / 4000 for an 8-page newsletter.

## EcoMark Solar Newsletter

Automated:
1. Set up auto email on Thursday morning soliciting contributions

Process:
1. Copy the template(https://docs.google.com/document/d/1exjP26jv8neUJEFC1xrCF-9eGyWyoyBL_IgyFvpyohA/edit)
2. Update shortcut link
3. Add to template throughout the week
4. Copy repeatable content from previous week
5. Do the creative writing bit
6. Bold all of the employee names to make them stand out
7. Review
  1. Print and proofread the newsletter
  2. Send to HR@ecomarksolar.com censors
  3. Make any necessary edits
8. Send!
