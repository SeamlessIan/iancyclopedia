### Plan 2: Steamed Veggies & Quinoa
#### The veggies
1. buy all the veggies that are on sale
2. Dice them and put them in containers
3. steam them in the logical order based on time to cook: 
(source: http://www.geniuskitchen.com/recipe/healthy-steamed-vegetables-241260)
  1. Add 1 1/2" water to the bottom of the pot
  2. Set steamer in the bottom of the pot 
  3. Heat over medium-high heat until water is boiling.
  4. Deposit vegetables with a light salting and garlic to taste; season salt may be used, to taste.
  5. Replace lid and steam until vegetables are tender, but not mushy. (Fork will insert easily, but will not tear veggies apart, when done.)
  6. Remove to a bowl and serve as is.

#### How long to steam different things
Carrots, Potatoes, Turnips, Squash: 8 to 20 minutes
Kale and Collards: 10 minutes
Broccoli Florets, Cauliflower Florets, Green Beans: 5 to 7 minutes
Spinach and Arugula: 3 minutes
Peas: 3 minutes

### Linguine with Shrimp and Plum Tomatoes
I've made this fancy meal during date night with Tina.

Source: http://www.epicurious.com/recipes/food/views/linguine-with-shrimp-and-plum-tomatoes-104810
#### INGREDIENTS
- 2 pounds uncooked large shrimp, peeled, deveined, tails left intact
- 10 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 pound assorted wild mushrooms (such as crimini, baby portable, and stemmed shiitake), sliced
- 4 pounds plum tomatoes, seeded, chopped (about 7 1/2 cups)
- 1 1/2 cups chopped fresh basil
- 1 1/2 cups plus 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 6 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper (optional)
- 1 pound feta cheese, crumbled (about 2 1/2 cups)
- 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 1/2 pounds linguine

#### PREPARATION

Place shrimp in medium bowl; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Heat 3 tablespoons oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp; sautÃ© until cooked through, about 3 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer to plate; cover. Add 4 tablespoons oil to same skillet. Add mushrooms; sautÃ© until tender, about 8 minutes. Add tomatoes, basil, 1 1/2 cups parsley, garlic, and red pepper, if desired; stir until heated through. Mix in both cheeses.

Meanwhile, cook linguine in large pot of boiling salted water until tender but still firm to bite. Drain. Return to pot. Add 3 tablespoons oil; toss to coat. Add mushroom-tomato mixture to pasta; toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer linguine to large bowl. Top with shrimp and 2 tablespoons parsley.
