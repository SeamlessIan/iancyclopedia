## Interview Questions
- If you think that a co-worker or your manager is doing something incorrectly, or that there is a better way of doing things, would you bring it to their attention? If so, how?
- Tell me about the tools or systems that you use to manage your workload.
- Do you want this job? Why?
- Can you tell me about a time at school or work when you led or managed a team?
