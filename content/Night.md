At night, I like to reflect on the preceding day and prepare for the new one.

## Nightly ritual
- Prepare fresh fruits and vegetables for the following day
- Review [monthly plan](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1nGfFmdDkCq26WI0Dwol6_sPbkQkQIkjQjH1esnKe5Zw/edit#gid=3370314)
- Review [expenses & budget](https://www.mint.com/)
- Plan next day
  - Review calendar(http://calendar.google.com)
  - Set alarm
  - Lay out clothing for following day
  - Envision it all
- Put away dishes
- Drink & get H2O
- Self
  - Floss
  - Wash face
  - Brush teeth
  - Mouthwash
  - Take vitamins
- Plug in phone somewhere besides my bedroom
- Put away used clothing/close open loops
- Update Lights(https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Dx2SZRlOX9BopFEkvxwaZe9UNLUgcd7WGEiyut78qC0/edit#gid=1031876495)
