---
title: How to Ask a Question in a Group Setting
date: 2017-08-30
---

Wrong:
- Be concise
- Only ask questions that will be generally relevant

Right:
- Start with a long personal anecdote
- Make sure that the question is extremely specific to you and not relevant to anyone else in the group
- If you only want a platform to give your own personal unsolicited opinion, end with a long pause, and then "...what are your thoughts on that?"
