---
title: The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up
---

Read October 2017

Takeaways:
- Take the time to tidy once and for all, by
  - Disposing of everything that doesn't inspire joy
  - Finding a proper place for everything
- Fold things by gently rolling them and then store them next to each other, rather than stacking them in a pile. In fact, never pile things.

My only complaint is that I had to skip part of the intro because, like many of these pop self-help books, the author takes a disproportionate amount of time and words explaining how great the method is and how much it will change a person'd life, instead of just getting straight to the content of the book.
