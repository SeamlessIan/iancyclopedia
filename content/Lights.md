Lights habit tracking was invented by Sebastian Marshall. [Here is his spreadsheet generator](https://ultra.work/lights2).

## Making new Lights Spreadsheet
This takes about 15 minutes.
1. Generate new spreadsheet
2. Add to it, if I hit 70% on the last one, or simplify, if not
3. Add at least 1 new challenge
4. Define my Most Important Work
