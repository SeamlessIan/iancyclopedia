## Unofficial Guide
- You can turn on/off subtotals for each row/column by right-clicking on the row/column header. This is not described in any official support documentation to my knowledge.

## Reports checklist
- What is the purpose of this report?
- Is this report accessible to all those who need it?
  - Sent out automatically & periodically?
  - Log in / URL / other access as needed?
- Are ALL relevant data columns shown? Are ONLY relevant data columns shown?
- Is the data organized in a way that is most conducive to the report's purpose?
