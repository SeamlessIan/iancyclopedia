---
title: Eating chicken
---

[Category:Scripts]

Setting: A chicken farmer's backyard.

Chicken-Eater (CE): I would like to buy a chicken.

Farmer (F): That will be seven dollars please.

CE hands F seven dollars. F hands CE a chicken.

Camera rotates to a stump. At this stump, CE decapitates the chicken.

Short montage of a chicken being cleaned and cooked.

Narrator (N): Something much worse than this happens every time you eat a chicken.
