This is a list I use when cleaning my bathroom, or better yet, when paying someone else to do it.

Bathroom:
- Empty everything from room
- Spray everything
- Wipe down everything with wet rags
- Clean toilet bowl
- Replace towel
- Supply
  - Toilet paper
  - Hand soap
