This is a lesson plan template based on the template used by the US Army: http://www.me.ngb.army.mil/units/rti/resources/ABIC_lesson_plan_template.pdf

## Lesson Title
1. Section 1: Overview
  1. Tasks taught & reinforced
  2. Time required for this lesson
  3. References
  4. Materials required
  5. Training space requirements
2. Section 2: Introduction
  1. Motivator
  2. Terminal Learning Objective
    1. Action
    2. Condition
    3. Standard
  3. Evaluation
3. Section 3: Presentation
  1. Learning Step / Activity 1
    1. One-sentence summary
    2. Method of instruction
    3. Time of instruction
    4. Media/Reference
    5. Summary
  2. Learning Step / Activity 2 
    1. Include same from Activity 1 
4. Section 4: Summary
  1. Check on Learning By:
    1. Soliciting student questions and explanations
    2. Asking questions and getting answers from students
    3. Providng immediate feedback in context to the material presented and correcting student misunderstandings
  2. Summarize Lesson
  3. Transition to Next Lesson
5. Section 5: Student Evaluation
  1. How will students demonstrate accomplishment of the TLO (Terminal Learning Objective)?
    1. Written test
    2. Completion of activity
  2. Schedule follow-up and additional training if needed
