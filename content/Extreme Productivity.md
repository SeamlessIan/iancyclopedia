---
title: Extreme Productivity
---

Big ideas:
1. Articulate your goals and rank them in order of priority
  1. Write down everything you are doing or are planning to do to achieve goals
  2. Organize goals by time horizon
  3. Rank objectives by relative importance
  4. Rank targets by relative importance
  5. Estimate how I actually spend my time & compare to my objectives
  6. Understand & address reasons for mismatches between goals & time allocations
2. Focus on the final product
3. Don't sweat the small stuff
