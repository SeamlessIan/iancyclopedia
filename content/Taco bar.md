---
title: Taco bar
---

For when you want to have people over for dinner but don't feel like cooking all afternoon.
## Ingredients
- Rotisserie chicken. It's ok, chickens don't really have feelings.
- Beans
- Rice
- Hard shells
- Soft shells
- lettuce
- tomato 
- onion
- Cilantro
- Guacamole
- Salsa
- Sour Cream
- Cheese
- Jalapenos

## Procedure
Cut everything up and put it out and make people make their own tacos.
