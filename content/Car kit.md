title: Car kit
---

[Category:Checklists]

At some point, you'll be glad you have these items in your car or you'll wish that you had.

## Trunk
- Jumper cables
- Jacks
- Tarp
- Blanket(s)
- Wrenches
- Glass cleaner
- Washer fluid
- Antifreeze/radiator fluid
- Boots
- Rope
- Shovel multi-tool (can't find this Chinese SOB)
- Gas can
- H2O

## Glove Box
- Gloves (obviously, otherwise you just have a box)
- Tire pressure gauge
- Proof of insurance
- Registration
- Kleenex
- Trash bags
- Napkins
- Matches/lighter (not to be used with gas can)
