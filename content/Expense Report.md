---
title: Expense Report
---

[Category:Checklists]

Every month, Accounting sends a spreadsheet of the previous month's credit card expenses, and we need to turn in receipts and details for each expense. Here is the process to follow to do this.

1. In Google Sheets, copy the previous month's expense report
2. Rename the copy with the current month's name
3. Delete the previous month's data from the sheet
4. Accounting will have sent one or more emails with the month's expenses. Copy the data from this email into the spreadsheet
5. Print all the receipts
6. Categorize all the expenses
7. Print the expense report
8. Fasten the report & receipts together with a paperclip or binder clip
9. Turn it in
