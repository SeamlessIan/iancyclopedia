I keep this bag in my car in case I decide I want to run, explore a neighborhood on foot, go to the gym, etc.

- Shorts/pants
- T-Shirt
- Bandana
- Underwear
- Socks
- Running shoes
- Gloves
- Camelbak
- Headphones
- Towel
- Frisbee/football/similar item
