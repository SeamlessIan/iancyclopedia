## Times I was Worried Unnecessarily about Dying
- June, 2017: Found a dark blue blood vein about 1" long in my right calf, which felt indented when I ran my finger over it. Thought I have deep vein thrombosis and was in imminent danger of a blood clot coming dislodged and causing me a stroke or heart attack. According to a doctor, I do not yet need to be worried about my own impending mortality.

## Injuries
Saturday, March 3, 2018: While rollerblading, fell about 5 times. The last time, I hit the ground hard to the amused horror of 5 neighborhood kids sitting on their porch. Several weeks later, when the pain hadn't yet subsided, I went to the doctor for an X-ray (inconclusive) and a wrist brace (also inconclusive). The possible diagnosis is a Navicular (Scaphoid) Fracture of the wrist. Outcome TBD.
