title: Best practices
---

## Speech
- Say the subject and object
- Don't omit words or abbreviate
- Take your time

## Writing
- Outline first
- First draft, go for quantity over quality
- On revision, compress by 50%

## Work
- Focus time on individual activities in lengthly blocks

## Health
- Eat primarily fresh fruits and vegetables
- Floss teeth

## Art
- Write your ideas down
