The following is from [Guide to Landscape Design](Homeowners) by Timothy M. Michel.

The seven steps to the design process are:
1. Reading the Land: The search for Design clues
2. Preparing the Base Map
3. Identifying Existing Conditions
4. Deciding What You Want
5. Diagramming Activity Areas
6. Designing the Overall Scheme
7. Finishing the Design
