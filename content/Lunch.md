This is the best way I know of to do lunch if you are an office worker.

## Assumptions
- You have a refrigerator at your place of work
- You have the time and means to go to a grocery store during your lunch period

## Preparation
d = (days in workweek) - 1
1. take a cutting board and knife to work if these tools aren't provided
2. bring a book to read to the office
3. On Monday (or the first day of your workweek), drive to the nearest grocery and buy the following things:
4. * mixed greens
5. * sweet peppers
6. * d pieces of fruit
7. * d avocados
8. * 1 large container of mixed nuts
9. * 1 bag of pre-shredded carrots
10. * cheese (optional)
11. * vinegar and/or oil (optional)
12. * any additional vegetables that you like
13. Put in an online order at a Chipotle, to be picked up on your way back to the office. You're going to earn this indulgence by making your own lunches the rest of the week.

## Execution
The rest of the days of the week (Tues - Fri, for me), do the following:
1. Make a giant salad comprised of
2. * greens
3. * chopped up sweet peppers
4. * sliced avocado
5. * diced piece of fruit
6. * handful of shredded carrots
7. * mixed nuts
8. * Optional ingredients (optional)
9. Eat it
10. Read
11. Feel good about the fact that you just got all 5 fruits and veggies into your diet in a single meal
