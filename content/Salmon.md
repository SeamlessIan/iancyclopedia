title: Salmon
---

## Ingredients
- garlic
- lemon
- oil, oilve or sesame (optional)
- rosemary (optional)
- salmon
- ginger

## Instructions
### Marinade
1. cut garlic
2. cut ginger
3. juice lemon into bag
4. add oil
5. add rosemary
6. add garlic & ginger
7. add salmon

### Cook
- Grill, OR
- Cook in cast iron pan on stovetop
