Now is the only time you ever have. Stop trying to get to the future.

## Checklist
- Take a deep breath and calm your mind
- Close any easy open loops in your immediate vicinity
- Choose something to work on. If you don't know, how about the next item on the [Iancyclopedia] to-do list?
  - Or if at work, why not chip away at the weekly newsletter, or pick the next ticket in the queue?
- If you can't work because your brain is not serving you, then do whatever you can to help your future self
