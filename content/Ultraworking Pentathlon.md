Feedback:
- On day 2, the email came out saying that my score was 600/1200, and gave me a ranking as compared to the rest of the competitors. In my opinion, this wrong/incomplete information is worse than no information at all. Suggestion: Send this email out on the International Date Line midnight, or when the last competitor's bedtime has passed, so that all competitors are looking at the same thing.
- On the Arena page, it would be nice to have a pop-up or mouseover box for each section displaying the target.
- The clock doesn't allow typing in times. Also if you display it as 06:30 instead of 6:30, it will be more difficult to misunderstand
