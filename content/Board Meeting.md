A board meeting is an necessary part of a corporation's leadership. The purpose is to review the health of the company and plan the direction the company will move in.

[Board Meeting Minutes Template (Google Docs)](https://docs.google.com/document/d/16yaW6Axj62xzjJu0OVcmthPCvcZ10x-sXmBmaGGsw5g/edit?usp=sharing)

## Preparation
- Create an agenda
  - contact board members for agenda item submissions
  - collect reports
  - send agenda to board members before the meeting
  - Prepare an attendance sheet

## Conducting the meeting
- Call to order
- Sign attendance sheet
- Recording secretary reads the minutes from the previous meeting
- Ask board members to discuss any changes to the minutes
  - Move to amend or
  - Move to accept the minutes
- Board member / department reports
  - Finance
  - Sales
  - Marketing
  - IT
  - Operations

- Discuss any unfinished business from pervious meetings
- Discuss any new business on the agneda
- discuss any new business NOT on the agenda
- Move to adjourn/schedule next meeting
- Move to officially end the meeting
