Traction:

Put 50% of resources into product & 50% into traction. Engage with cold customers to find out where your product's defects lie ("holes in your bucket"). At first, much of this effort seems to be wasted (seep out of the holes), but as you plug the holes, your traction will increase and you'll retain more of the water.
