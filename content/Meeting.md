This is a checklist for use in scheduling, conducting, and following up on meetings.

## Preparation
1. Provide nametags. This facilitates discussion
2. Write meeting agenda ([template here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1itFhV9VZ355hYkos76FW0BUddNiZVqKBmh7OMdACYYE/edit#heading=h.mnxh04tp6wnh))
  1. Purpose
  2. Information to distribute
  3. Questions to resolve
  4. Section for action items
  5. Section for notes
3. Share/Print hard copy for each participant

## Running the meeting
1. Distribute agenda
2. Convey information
3. Resolve all open questions
4. Record all action items and deliverables offered or assigned
5. If topics of discussion arise that are outside of the scope of the meeting, schedule additional meetings for these topics if needed, and redirect the meeting back to the task at hand

## Follow up
1. Prepare meeting summary
  1. Resolved questions
  2. Action items / deliverables
2. Send meeting summary to all participants
3. Complete action items and deliverables
4. Schedule follow-up contact or meeting
