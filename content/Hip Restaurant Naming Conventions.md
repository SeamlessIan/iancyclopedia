---
title: "Hip Restaurant Naming Conventions"
date: 2017-03-06
---

In Denver, the way to name your hip new farm-to-table gastropub is to pick two nouns (proper or otherwise) and connect them with an ampersand, plus sign, or even the completely un-hip word "and".

- Work & Class
- Hutch & Spoon
- Beatrice & Woodsley
- Beast + Bottle
- Guard and Grace
- Colt & Gray
- The Pig & The Sprout
- Stoic & Genuine
