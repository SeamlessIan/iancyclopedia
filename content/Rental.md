### Cleaning Checklist
Because the washer/dryer and dishwasher are limited resources that act as bottlenecks, start by stripping the sheets and starting the laundry, then by washing the dishes, before continuing with anything else.
- Strip & wash:
  - sheets & pillowcases
  - rugs
  - kitchen rags and hand towels
  - bathroom towels & hand towels
- Run dishwasher
  - Collect all dirty dishes and move to dishwasher
  - Start dishwasher

After that, go to the back of the apartment and clean yourself right out the door.
#### Office
- strip/wash/replace sheets if bed used
- wipe surfaces (top to bottom)
  - windowsills
  - desk
  - end table
- vacuum rug/floor
- mop floor
#### Bathroom
- Remove everything
  - Towels / hand-towel (wash)
  - Trash can (empty)
  - Rug (vacuum; wash if needed)
  - Hand soap dispenser
  - Toilet brush / plunger
- Windex / wipe everything:
  - Mirror
  - Sink
  - Toilet
  - Shower
  - Floor (wipe/mop)
- Stock supplies
  - Toilet paper - min 2 extra rolls
  - Hand soap - fill dispenser
  - Towels - 1 per guest
  - Hand-towel - 1
  - Shampoo - minimum 1/3 bottle
  - Conditioner - minimum 1/3 bottle
  - Body wash - minimum 1/3 bottle
- Replace items
  - Rug
  - Toilet brush / plunger

#### Bedroom
- Strip sheets / wash them
- Clean surfaces (top to bottom)
  - Windowsills
  - Dresser
  - TV stand
  - Nightstands
  - Chest
- Vacuum rugs/floor
- Mop floor
- replace sheets

#### Living room
- Wipe surfaces (top to bottom)
  - Windowsill
  - Fireplace ledge
  - Dining room table
  - End table
  - Coffee Table
- Vacuum rug, and couch if needed
- Mop floor

#### Kitchen
- Remove hand towels / rags
- Clean fridge
  - Empty it
  - Wipe anything on the inside
- Wash dishes / put them away
- Wipe down surfaces (top to bottom)
  - Fridge
  - Windowsill
  - Counters
- Vacuum rug
- Mop floor
- Stock supplies
  - Ice
  - Coffee / tea
    - filters
  - Paper towels

#### General
- Trash
  - Empty vacuum dust collector into trash
  - Take out trash
  - Replace all bags
- Water plants
  - Kitchen
  - Living room
  - Bedroom
  - Office
- Reset door codes
