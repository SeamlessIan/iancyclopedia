---
title: Circumcision
---

Circumcision is the process of holding down a baby boy and cutting off part of his penis. It is usually done for authentic religious and dubious medical reasons.

Admittedly, my memory of the day I was circumcised is foggy as I was about a week old, but from what I can recall I wasn't particularly religious at that point in my life.

To my disappointment, I found out later that the process is not reversible.

Unlike piercings and tattoos, performing this type of extreme body modification on other people won't raise any eyebrows.
