by Eckhardt Tolle

Since starting this book, I notice about a dozen times per day when I'm thinking with my Ego (and what other way is there to think?).

Things my Ego likes to attach to:
- House
- Job title
- Salary
- Girls I'm dating
- Other people doing stupid things, and me not doing them by contrast
- Telling people what to do
- Whether or not people validate me when I seek it
- What I eat
- My opinions
- My habits
- This wiki

What is your purpose in life? Mine right now is to write this sentence. Your purpose, when reading it, is to read it.
