---
title: Webinar Checklist
---

Organizing a multi-person webinar - checklist. This checklist was made for Zoho Meeting webinars specifically, but can be easily adapted for other software.

- Schedule webinar
- Publish webinar sign-up link
- Pre-approve registrants if applicable
- If multiple presenters in multiple locations, clearly define...
  - Agenda
  - Transitions between multiple speakers/resources shown
  - backup communication channel for organizers (e.g. G-Chat).
- Test audio
- Start webinar
- Start screen recording
- After meeting, follow up with attendees, as well as those who registered & did not attend
